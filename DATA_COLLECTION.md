## DATA COLLECTION CHECKLIST

Before adding to the app, verify:

- [ ] Organization name is correct
- [ ] Address is complete and accurate
- [ ] Phone number verified
- [ ] Hours are current (many changed during COVID)
- [ ] Service type is correct
- [ ] Coordinates found on Google Maps
- [ ] Description is clear and helpful
- [ ] Special notes are included

---

## PHONE SCRIPT

Use this when calling organizations:

---

**"Hi! I'm calling on behalf of [Your Organization Name]. We're building a free online map to help community members find local food resources. Would you be willing to share information about the services you offer? It would only take about 5 minutes."**

If yes, continue:

1. "What type of service do you provide?" (food boxes/meals/soup kitchen)
2. "What are your hours of operation?" (get each day)
3. "Is there anything people should know before visiting?" (registration, first-come-first-served, etc.)
4. "Any other information you'd like included?"

---

## TIPS FOR DATA COLLECTION

✅ **DO:**
- Be polite and brief
- Call during business hours
- Have a pen ready
- Save completed forms
- Double-check information

❌ **DON'T:**
- Call during meal times
- Be demanding
- Pressure for information
- Share private information
- Forget to thank them
